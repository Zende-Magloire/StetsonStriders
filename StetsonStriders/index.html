Stetson Striders

Stetson Striders is a student-led Club Sport committed to bringing the joy of track to Stetson University. We strive to encourage runners of any level to improve and work towards their running goals. The love of track will be displayed through joint meets with AAU, USATF, and NIRCA. With each coming year we plan to emphasize the importance of health, wellness, and fitness.
